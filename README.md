Mazerunner: A maze game with interactable doors and keys. <br>
Controls: <br>
Player Movement: WASD <br>
Camera: Mouse <br>
Pause Menu: ESC <br>
Doors will open if you collect their respective coloured keys and walk near the door. <br>
